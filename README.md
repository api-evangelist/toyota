# Toyota (toyota)
Toyota Motor Corporation is a Japanese multinational automotive manufacturer that designs, manufactures, and sells vehicles, including cars, trucks, and buses. Founded in 1937, Toyota has become one of the largest automakers in the world, known for its reliability, innovation, and commitment to sustainability. Toyota's product lineup includes a wide range of vehicles, from fuel-efficient hybrids like the Prius to rugged trucks like the Tacoma. In addition to producing vehicles, Toyota also operates financial services, research and development centers, and manufacturing plants around the globe. The company is dedicated to creating safe, high-quality vehicles that meet the needs of customers worldwide.
